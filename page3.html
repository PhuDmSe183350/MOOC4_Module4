Vietnamese Food Tour

Embark on a culinary journey through Vietnam and discover the diverse flavors and dishes that make Vietnamese cuisine so unique.

Phở

Phở is a famous Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat, usually beef (phở bò) or chicken (phở gà). It's a staple of Vietnamese cuisine and is enjoyed at any time of day.

Bánh Mì

Bánh mì is a Vietnamese sandwich that consists of a crispy baguette filled with various ingredients such as pork, pate, mayonnaise, pickled vegetables, and fresh herbs. It's a perfect blend of flavors and textures.

Hủ Tiếu

Hủ Tiếu is a versatile noodle soup that can be made with various types of noodles, including clear, egg, or rice noodles. It is often served with a mix of pork, seafood, and vegetables, making it a hearty and satisfying meal.
